OUR MISSION

Empower girls to pursure STEM

During our time in high school robotics, we noticed ongoing issues of gender diversity and bias towards female members of the club. Many girls left the team because they felt singled-out or under-recognized. Realizing that this was an issue in our community, we launched STEMpowher to build an encouraging network for girls in STEM.
