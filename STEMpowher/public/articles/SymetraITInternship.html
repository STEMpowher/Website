SYMETRA - Summer 2021 Information Technology Internship Program



Symetra has an exciting opportunity to be a part of their 2021 Information Technology Internship Program this summer!



ABOUT THE PROGRAM

There are several exciting opportunities in the Information Technology organization where you can help them pave the way for the digital experience at Symetra. During the program you'll gain practical experience, skills, and knowledge you can use to build a career in the Tech world. Selected interns will be matched to internships based on area of interest, aptitude, and areas of study.

They are looking to fill 12 positions in the IT Internship Program for Summer 2021. The internship is a paid, full time opportunity for three months (12 weeks). If you're looking to learn, collaborate and make an impact, this is the program for you!

REQUIREMENTS

  • Majoring in computer science, management information systems or another technology degree
  • Willingness to learn and self-starter
  • Strong analytical, problem solving, and communication skills
  • Ability to work collaboratively with others
    •

PROJECT EXAMPLES

Software Test Engineer - Work with the FAST Policy Admin System team and the system vendor to execute test cases for new products and features, and data integrations

Software Development Engineer - Develop, populate, and retrieve data from a new Operational Data Store (ODS)/Data Lake

Software Developer - Integration Services: Works in DevOps environment building delivery pipelines, application code, automated tests, and monitoring solutions.

Software Development Engineer in Test - Contribute to building the user Interface automation test suite, along with identifying areas where we can build integration test suite

Quality Analyst and Operations Engineer - Ensure that our desktop experience is managed securely, with timely planned updates and minimal disruption to our users

IT Risk and Security Analyst - Investigate and respond to IT security alerts, contribute to improvements of the IT risk and controls program

IT Digital Transformation Solution Architect - Participate in Solution Design Support in tandem with our Solution Architects and support the AWS infrastructure
